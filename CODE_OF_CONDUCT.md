# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

Please read the full text at the link above. By participating in this project, you agree to abide by its terms.

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at the email address listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.
